Our Story

Our story starts with my daughter Pauline and her love for animals. I personally remember how much my dog meant to me growing up and I wanted to give Pauline that same feeling. I wanted to do more then just adopt or buy from breeder. There is no better feeling then saving dogs.

When we adopted Cookie during the start of the pandemic she truly saved us more than we saved her. She was the opposite of outgoing - she was shut down in the shelter, barely acknowledging me, we I took Pauline to visit with her, she seen Pauline and right on the spot on she leaned in slightly and snuggles with her, the deal was sealed.

We want to bring awareness to all the shelter Doggo's deserving and in need of homes. It's always the perfect time to celebrate the joys of adopting a dog.
